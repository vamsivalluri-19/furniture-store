Legendary Durability

Burmese Teak (Tectona grandis)

Rich in natural rubber and oils, Burmese Teak offers unmatched moisture resistance and dimensional stability. It resists rotting, insect attacks, and warping, making it the most coveted timber for luxury indoor and outdoor furniture.

Hardness (Janka)
1,070 lbf
Grain Pattern Straight, coarse texture, slightly greasy feel
Natural Color Golden brown to amber tones

Royal Elegance

Honduran Mahogany (Swietenia macrophylla)

Mahogany is a world-renowned cabinet wood prized for its workability, smooth finish, and deep reddish-brown luster. It displays exceptional dimensional stability and yields a stunning, reflective surface when polished.

Hardness (Janka)
900 lbf
Grain Pattern Interlocking or straight, medium texture, highly uniform
Natural Color Reddish-brown, darkens over time

Classic Strength

European White Oak (Quercus robur)

White Oak is an exceptionally hard, heavy, and rot-resistant hardwood. Highly favored in traditional and contemporary craft, its prominent rays and rings produce striking grain patterns (especially when quartersawn).

Hardness (Janka)
1,360 lbf
Grain Pattern Straight, uneven texture with ray-fleck markings
Natural Color Light to medium brown with olive casts
Browse Oak Products
Grain Showcase
Luxury Choice

American Walnut (Juglans nigra)

American Black Walnut is the pinnacle of luxury cabinet timber. Rich dark brown tones, sometimes with purplish streaks, provide a warm and luxurious presence. Its fine texture responds magnificently to natural wax finishes.

Hardness (Janka)
1,010 lbf
Grain Pattern Usually straight, can be wavy, with medium texture
Natural Color Deep chocolate brown with lighter sapwood
Browse Walnut Products
Grain Showcase
Dramatic Luster

Indian Rosewood (Dalbergia sissoo)

Also known as Sheesham, Indian Rosewood is highly prized for its extreme density and contrasting grain stripes. Extremely tough and heavy, Rosewood furniture is incredibly resistant to wear and carries a unique presence.

Hardness (Janka)
1,660 lbf
Grain Pattern Interlocking, highly textured with bold streaks
Natural Color Golden brown to deep chestnut with dark streaks
